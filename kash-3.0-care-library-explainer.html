"The library" = your shelf of small self-care practices

Same idea as Finch's self-care list. It has two halves: a suggested catalog we seed, and your own practices. This is the Tasks tab inside Care.

A practice is one small repeatable act — "take a 10-min walk," "box breathing," "drink a glass of water," "write one line." You adopt ones you like from the suggested catalog, or create your own. The ones you keep become your list, which you check off.
