Paired toggle — auto-scroll to “now”: independent of the window choice. For a living record that updates through the day, opening already parked at the current time (so the next thing is in view) is the natural default. A & B especially benefit (they have off-screen hours); C & D effectively bake it in. Recommend: auto-scroll ON, with a “jump to now” affordance if you scroll away. You decide.
