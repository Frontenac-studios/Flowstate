Tokens · A1 — Multi-project calendar: project-mode coloring

Project-mode must distinguish projects without reading as a category. The catch: categories already own most hues.

The wheel is nearly claimed. Blue (Pro), Purple (Personal), Red (Rel), Orange (Adulting), Yellow (reserved), Green (Body) leave only thin gaps (teal, indigo, magenta, brown). Any project hue risks colliding with a category — which is why non-hue schemes are worth a hard look.
